Shuffle Or Repeat Playback

Toggle the Shuffle option On to playback songs in your list in a random order. Alternatively, toggle the Shuffle option Off to playback songs in the sequential order they are listed in.

Toggle the Repeat option On to repeat playback of the playlist, from the start, when it has finished playing the last song. Alternatively toggle the Repeat option Off to complete playback when the last song has finished playing.

Both the Shuffle and Repeat playback options can be accessed from the context menu.
