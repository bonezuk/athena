License and Registration

Black Omega is a shareware and you must purchase a license in order to continue using it after your initial 30 day trial period. You can purchase a license from the Black Omega website.

Once you have purchased a license your license registration code will be emailed to the email address given by you at the time of purchase.

To enter your license registration code open the license dialog box.

  1. Clicking the License button at the bottom.
  2. About Black Omega from the Playlist Context Menu

Now type in your registration code into the license dialog. You can also Copy the registration code from the email and Paste it in via the Paste button on the registration dialog.

Click the Register button to authenticate and license you copy of Black Omega.

When you purchase a license you can specify the number of computers you wish to run Black Omega on. Every time you register and authenticate a license it is tied to that computer. Thus you cannot buy a license for a single computer and use that same license on more than one computer.

If you wish to move your license to use it on another computer then you can go into the License dialog and click Deactivate. This will unregister the copy of your license from that computer and free your license code up to be used on another computer.
